About ChessWorld.com

Welcome to ChessWorld.com, your ultimate destination for an exhilarating chess experience! Founded by Tanmay Bisht, a passionate computer science student at IIT-BHU, ChessWorld.com is the culmination of dedication, creativity, and a love for the game of chess.

Our Mission

At ChessWorld.com, our mission is simple yet profound: to provide a platform where players of all levels can come together to enjoy the timeless game of chess. We aim to foster a vibrant community of chess enthusiasts, offering them the tools and resources they need to improve their skills, challenge themselves, and connect with fellow players from around the globe.

What We Offer

  • Engaging Gameplay
  • Two-Player Mode
  • Varied Time Formats
  • Educational Resources

About the Founder

Tanmay Bisht, the brain behind ChessWorld.com, is a dedicated student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi. With a passion for technology and a love for chess, Tanmay embarked on the journey to create ChessWorld.com as his first major project in the field of web development.
